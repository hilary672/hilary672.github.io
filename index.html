So, about me...

Female. Badass. I spend my nine‑to‑five designing for Edison Software, headquartered in Silicon Valley. After hours I love working with other brands, and clients of all backgrounds. Follow @lefthook.design on Instagram for my recent projects. The rest of my time is spent in the beautiful Pacific Northwest (just outside Portland, OR). Home is where my pets are (dogs and cats) and I'm an outdoor junky who loves unplugging to spend some time in nature. Always active, I grew up in the Northern California Mountains backpacking, hiking, skiing, and swimming. The only sport I follow religiously is motorcycle racing, and I learned to ride my own dirtbike when I was eleven. I believe that great things never came from comfort zones, and good things come to those who hustle.
